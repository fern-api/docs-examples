## Quickstart

<Steps>

<Step title="Pick a variety">
Browse the variety sub-sites above to find planting guides, soil requirements, and expected yields.
</Step>

<Step title="Place an order">
Use the `POST /plants/{plantId}/order` endpoint to submit a bulk seed order. Each order requires a valid greenhouse destination.
</Step>

<Step title="Track germination">
Monitor germination progress through the dashboard or subscribe to webhook events for status updates.
</Step>

</Steps>
