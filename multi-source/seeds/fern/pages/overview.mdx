The Seeds team manages the seed catalog, germination protocols, and supplier integrations. This documentation covers how to browse the catalog, place bulk orders, and track germination rates.

## Quickstart

<Steps>

<Step title="Browse the catalog">
Open the [seed catalog](/seeds/getting-started/seed-catalog) to search varieties by climate zone, germination time, or plant family.
</Step>

<Step title="Place an order">
Use the `/plants/{plantId}/order` endpoint to submit a bulk seed order. Each order requires a valid greenhouse destination.
</Step>

<Step title="Track germination">
Monitor germination progress through the dashboard or subscribe to webhook events for status updates.
</Step>

</Steps>
