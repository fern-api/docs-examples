The Nursery team handles everything after germination — transplanting seedlings, managing grow-out schedules, and preparing plants for distribution. This documentation covers the nursery workflow from intake to shipment.

## Quickstart

<Steps>

<Step title="Register seedlings">
When seedlings arrive from the Seeds team, register them with `POST /nursery/intake` including the seed lot ID and greenhouse destination.
</Step>

<Step title="Assign grow-out schedules">
Each plant variety has a recommended grow-out schedule. The system suggests one based on the species, but you can customize timing and nutrient plans.
</Step>

<Step title="Prepare for distribution">
Once plants reach maturity, mark them ready for distribution. The logistics system handles routing to retail locations or direct-to-customer shipments.
</Step>

</Steps>
