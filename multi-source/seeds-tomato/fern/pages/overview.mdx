## Germination specs

- **Soil temperature**: 21-27 C
- **Depth**: 6 mm
- **Spacing**: 60-90 cm apart (after transplant)
- **Germination time**: 5-10 days
- **Light**: Full sun (8+ hours)
- **Start indoors**: 6-8 weeks before last frost
